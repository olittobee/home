About
Skillzcamp

Skillzcamp is an EdTech that aims to bridge the gap between the current competence of undergraduates and graduates with the required skills to survive in various industries. Skillz camp started in 2019 with physical sessions. Programs that were taught ranged from the use of Microsoft Office Tools to Business Plan Development. Currently, we are expanding our programs into Data Analytics, Product Management, UX Design, Web monetization, copywriting among many others.
